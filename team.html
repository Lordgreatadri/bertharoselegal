Profile of Johnnie Klutse (Managing Partner)

Johnny Klutse is the managing partner at the Firm. He is a transactional and advocacy lawyer and has had considerable experience in Banking, Labour, Mining, and Energy sectors as well as commercial and personal litigation. Johnny has advised private clients, companies, and individuals, as well as various government entities, including regulators.

Johnny holds an LL.B degree from the University of Ghana and was called to the Ghanaian Bar in September 2011. He also holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in History and Philosophy from the University of Ghana.

Johnny is a member of the Ghana Bar Association and has eleven years of post-call experience in Ghana. He also had a brief secondment at the Dubai offices of Brian Cave Leighton Paisner (formerly Berwin Leighton Paisner) in 2017.

Profile of Johnnie Klutse (Managing Partner)

Seyram is a transactional lawyer with a specialization in Finance, Construction, and Investment law. In his time as a lawyer with the Electricity Company of Ghana (ECG), he led the Project Management Office Legal Team for the Millennium Challenge Compact-driven private sector participation transaction between ECG and PDS (ECG PSP) and also served as a member of the Government of Ghana Joint CP Committee in respect of the said transaction. He has also structured and negotiated several complex energy transactions including EPC contracts and PPAs.

Seyram holds an LLM from Columbia University in the City of New York with a concentration on Energy and Investment. Seyram was called to the Ghana Bar in the year 2011 after receiving his Qualifying Certificate in Law in the same year. He obtained his LLB from the University of Ghana and also holds a BSc. Biochemistry degree from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology.

Seyram is a holder of the Ghana Securities Industry certificate having passed all the courses at the Ghana Stock Exchange. He also attended the International Contracts School in Dubai and the Institute for Private Public Partnership (IP3) in Virginia.
